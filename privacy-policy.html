Overview

种菜日历 Veggie Calendar ("the App") is a planting calendar and seed guide for Chinese vegetables. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you enter (such as your city or zip code for local climate lookup) is stored exclusively on your own device and is never sent to us or any third party.

The App does not require account registration or login.

Location Information

You may optionally enter a city name or zip code to receive localized planting recommendations. This information is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to any server.

Device Permissions

The App may request access to device storage solely to save your preferences locally. No other device permissions are required.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Advertisements

The App does not display any advertisements.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no content inappropriate for children.
